SONOSOPHY

Sound Culture

Sonosophy is the philosophy of sound - the nature, existence and importance of sound in our life , it is an auditory
experience that fosters awareness and an understanding in our perception of sound.
Sound affects us based on What are we listening , Where are we listening and How are we listening.

Sonosophy's Objectives are to tell stories through sounds, to explore the science and art of sound through technology and
ancient ways. This is a platform where we are always in an excited state to work with sounds in any vertical - be it branding
through Sonic Logos or by designing soundscapes for various spaces or by exploring sound art and studying the acoustic
ecology.

We are trying to intensity the second most important sense out of the five senses i.e.,
Hearing
- by making spaces sound better.
- by fostering awareness the difference between music and noise.
- by encouraging new ways of experiencing music and sounds through our custom designed events.

Our network of music instructors, musicians, sound artists, acousticians, sound system integrators, enable us to move
closer to our objective of having a sonically fit society.

AMBIENT SESSION

Ambient session is for creating a space for you sonically and taking you into a journey of soundscapes and unexplored dimensions of mind.
Ambient music can fuel our productivity , creativity and imagination , can induce calmness and relaxation. We all know how music affects us psychologically , behaviourally . Ambient music becomes background and foreground whenever we want , which helps you to focus on your work , if you are reading , thinking , daydreaming , it gives you a space to enhance your activities . It can mask noise around you , it can heal you and many things which cannot be put into words.
